Dokokin wannan Qungiya

  • (1) Wajibi ne zartar da aiki a duk sanda ƙungiya ta saka ki ko ta wakilta ki akai.

  • (2) Yin shiru na dogon lokaci ba tare da miƙa uziri zai iya jawo hukunci ko kora daga ƙungiya.

  • (3) Lefi ne yin rubutu na ƙashin kai wanda ya saɓa da ƙa'idojin ƙungiya kuma a sanya hashtag ɗin Habibtys.

  • (4) . Kame kai a sarari da social media wajibi ne ga duk wata ƴar ƙungiya.

  • (5) Sanya Hoto me shiga ta rashin kamala haramun ne a ƙungiyar mu koda kuwa a kashin kai mutum yasa.

  • (6) A kiyayi yawan magana kan duk trending topics ɗin social media, mu taƙaita iyakar iyawar mu.

  • (7) Kiyaye wasa da wargi da shiga abunda bai shafe mu ba musamman a media dole ne, za'a Samar da masu lura da dukkan motsin mu don temaka mana mu gudu tare mu tshira tare.

  • (8) Wajibi ne zama friends din juna a Facebook da following juna domin ƙara kusanci da juna, ƙarfafa zumunci da mu'amala mekyau tsakanin mu. Idan anyi saving numbers ɗin juna hakan yana da kyau, amma ba wajibi bane ba.

  • (9) Idan kun samu matsala da wata a cikin Habibtys ki sanar da mu da gaggawa don a dau mataki akan kari, daukar mataki da kai ko yanke alaqa ko yada zance a gefe wannan ya sabawa dokokin kungiya.

  • (10) Comments a shafin mu na Safinatun Najati Habibtys, da karfafar juna da comments da likes idan mutum yayi posting dole ne.

  • (11) Wajibi ne sanar da Monitor uzuri idan ba zaki samu damar halartar karatu ko gabatar da wani abu da aka saka kiyi ba!

  • (12) Halartar dukkan wani meeting da za ai da bada shawarwari ko kawo gyara akan ko wace ce a cikinmu, sanann dole ne ya kasance kowa a cikinmu a shirye take don karba da kawo gyara idan taga kuskure ko tayi kuskure (komin matsayinta a kungiya dole a yi mata gyara kuma ta karba).

  • Note: Kiyaye dukkanin dokokin kungiya ba zabi bane ko ra'ayi dole ne akan kowacce mamba.
